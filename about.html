ABOUT US

Carbon emissions result from human activities that release carbon dioxide (CO2) and other greenhouse gases into the atmosphere, primarily from burning fossil fuels and deforestation. These emissions contribute significantly to global warming and climate change, impacting our planet's biodiversity and weather patterns. Our Personal Carbon Calculator helps estimate your carbon footprint based on daily activities like commuting, electricity use, waste generation, and diet. Understanding your impact empowers you to make sustainable choices and reduce your environmental footprint. Start using our calculator today to take meaningful steps towards a greener and more sustainable future!
